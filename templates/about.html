About TriviaFlick

🎬 Welcome to TriviaFlick – the ultimate challenge for movie lovers and trivia enthusiasts! Think you’re a true cinephile? Put your movie knowledge to the test as you decode cryptic hints, and guess the right movie.

The fewer hints you need, the more points you score – and only the sharpest minds will climb their way to the top of the leaderboard. Compete against friends or test your skills solo.?

✨ How It Works

  • Each round starts with a complex hint about a movie.
  • Not sure? Ask question about movie to guess it – but remember, fewer questions mean more points!
  • Submit your guess and rack up points to dominate the leaderboard.

🏆 Point System

  • Guessed by just provided Hint: 100 points
  • 1 Question asked: 90 points
  • 2 Question asked: 80 points
  • 9 or 9+ question asked: 10 points

The faster you crack the code, the higher you score. Are you ready to challenge yourself, outwit others, and secure your spot on the leaderboard?
